Our Soulful Menu

Made fresh. Made with love. Made for you.

Starters & Appetizers

  • Cheeseburger Eggroll — Multi-cheese crunchy beef eggroll, fried perfectly, served with chipotle aioli.
  • Hot Cheeto Chicken Balls — Chicken and cheese rolled with peppers, coated & dipped in batter, fried to perfection.
  • Quesadillas — Grilled with three cheeses and peppers. Ooey, gooey, and unforgettable.
  • Meatballs — Juicy baked meatballs with a Southern twist.

Main Courses

  • Fried Chicken — Buttermilk fried golden chicken, crispy on the outside, juicy on the inside.
  • Fried Fish — Fresh whiting fish dipped in our Cajun fish fry batter and fried to perfection.
  • Fried Jumbo Shrimp — Jumbo shrimp perfectly fried, also available in buffalo.
  • Jerk Rasta Pasta — Creamy, spicy pasta packed with island flavor. Choose chicken, shrimp, or steak.
  • Baked Smothered Turkey Wings — Slow-cooked turkey wings with delicious country gravy (or jerk sauce on request).
  • Lamb Chop — Grass-fed lamb, seasoned and seared with red wine.

Sides

  • Baked Beans — Savory beans, slow-cooked with ground beef & spices for a bold kick!
  • Mac & Cheese — Heavenly cheesy macaroni with a golden crust.
  • Green Beans — Fresh-snapped, slow-cooked in smoked turkey.
  • Mash Potatoes — Creamy garlic mashed potatoes made with Irish butter.
  • Broccoli — Fresh, sautéed with salt, pepper, garlic, and butter.
  • Loaded Baked Potatoes — Jumbo baked potato with salt, pepper, sour cream, cheese, bacon bites & green onion (add steak if you like).
  • Asparagus — Sautéed in garlic lemon herb butter.
  • Collard Greens — Southern-style collard & kale greens, slow-cooked with smoked turkey.
  • Yellow Rice — Cooked down with red peppers for big flavor.
  • Cabbage — Southern cabbage, slow-cooked with smoked turkey.
  • Rolls — Fresh-baked and perfect for sopping up every last drop.

Desserts

  • Honey Strawberry Cornbread — Homemade cornbread with fresh strawberries and sweet glazed top.
  • Strawberry Shortcake — Homemade vanilla and strawberry cake with light sweet icing and wafer topper.
  • Lemon Blueberry Cupcakes — Homemade cupcakes with lemon, blueberry, and a vanilla drizzle.
