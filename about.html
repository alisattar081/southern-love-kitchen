Our Story

Southern Love Kitchen was born from a passion for soulful flavors, family tradition, and sharing good food with good people.

Chef Meechii started in a small kitchen, guided by family recipes and fueled by a dream: to bring the warmth and joy of authentic southern cooking to the community.

Every dish is made with love, patience, and pride—from perfectly crispy fried chicken to melt-in-your-mouth peach cobbler. Our mission is to serve every guest like family and deliver southern hospitality with every bite.

Meet Chef Meechii

Founder, chef, and storyteller—Meechii brings decades of culinary experience and a relentless love for soul food.
Whether cooking for neighbors or catering a crowd, Chef Meechii believes the best meals are those that make you feel at home.
