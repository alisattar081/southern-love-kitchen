Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, credit/debit cards, Zelle, and Cash App. Online payments coming soon!

Do you deliver?

Yes, we offer local delivery in the Alexandria area. Delivery fees may apply depending on your location.

Are your dishes gluten-free or vegan?

Many of our dishes can be made gluten-free or vegan. Check our menu for dietary labels or ask when you order!

How do I book catering?

Use our Catering & Events page to send an inquiry, or call/email us to discuss your needs!

Can I order for a large group?

Absolutely! We love feeding a crowd. Please contact us for custom quotes for big orders or events.

Do you have gift cards?

Gift cards will be available soon! Stay tuned for updates.
